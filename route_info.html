Your adventure awaits! Where will you begin?

The Loire river by bike is a 900-kilometer cycle route that connects Cuffy (near Nevers) to Saint-Brevin-les-Pins (Loire-Atlantique).

Stretching out over 900 km, the Loire River by Bike (La Loire à Vélo) is one of the most beautiful cycle routes in France. Much of the route crosses areas which are listed as UNESCO World Heritage. This is your call to adventure, culture, and pure enjoyment along the Loire River by Bike. Let's begin!
